About VM Studio

A decade of sound, shaped with purpose

Visithiran is a passionate and highly experienced music producer and school educator with over 10+ years of dedication to both music and teaching. His journey combines artistic creativity with academic discipline, making his approach unique and impactful.

As a school teacher, he has inspired and guided students not only in education but also in discovering their musical talents. His teaching experience brings structure, clarity and professionalism into every aspect of his music training.

At VM Studio, every project is crafted with professional quality — from music production, mixing and mastering to artist development and training. His work reflects not just technical skill, but emotional depth that connects with listeners.

Over the years, he has trained hundreds of students and helped aspiring artists turn their ideas into reality. Whether you are a beginner or a professional, VM Studio provides the right environment to create powerful music. If you're looking for studio-quality sound, creative direction and professional guidance, VM Studio is where your music evolves into something extraordinary.

Yes. The academy provides structured keyboard, tabla and drums training for beginners and advanced students of all ages.

Can VM Studio compose background scores and film songs?

Yes. Visithiran composes background scores, film songs and independent albums, with full production, mixing and mastering done in-house.

Absolutely. With over 10 years of teaching experience, Visithiran has trained hundreds of students from complete beginners to working professionals.
